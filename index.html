Mimperio

GeoInsight Platform for Real-Time Socioeconomic Intelligence

Mimperio is a data-driven platform integrating satellite imagery, anonymized mobility data, and survey-based inputs to generate high-frequency indicators of economic activity in underserved regions.

What It Does

The platform enables measurement of firm activity, informality, and local economic dynamics where traditional data sources are sparse, delayed, or unreliable.

Core Approach

• Satellite-based proxies for infrastructure and nightlight intensity
• Mobile network-derived movement patterns
• Survey-grounded calibration for causal inference
• Integration into real-time analytical pipelines

Applications

• SME performance tracking in emerging markets
• Informality and productivity measurement
• Policy evaluation under limited data environments
• Investment intelligence for frontier economies

Current Work

Ongoing research includes firm-level activity mapping in Kigali using combined survey and geospatial data, as well as modeling tax compliance under constrained enforcement environments.
